# Change Roles of Existing Team Members

***

## How to change roles of existing Team Member?

{% embed url="<https://youtu.be/5j4vRet56tg>" %}

***

To modify the role of a current team member,&#x20;

* Go to the "Team" section from the left panel and choose the team member whose role you intend to change
* Click on the three dots located at the right end of the agent&#x20;
* Select "**View Profile**" from the dropdown menu

* The profile panel will open on the right-hand side of the screen

### Change  Organization Role

* After opening the profile panel, click on the dropdown menu under "**Organization Role**"

  ![](https://2303112206-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2F53n17VnOICC1LtDqlENV%2Fuploads%2FSNjVVMHk8MVh1OkHf5Hd%2Fimage.png?alt=media\&token=b8485749-940c-479b-ad93-7ad382f47e83)\\

* Choose the role you wish to assign to the team member from the dropdown menu

* The role selection will be automatically saved once selected

<div align="left"><figure><img src="https://2303112206-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2F53n17VnOICC1LtDqlENV%2Fuploads%2FdWlpND28pZdb9jp8Lk79%2Fimage.png?alt=media&#x26;token=a5f39c04-fdc3-463c-a12a-079f9fb4ff83" alt="" width="410"><figcaption></figcaption></figure></div>

* The Organization role has been successfully updated from "**Owner**" to "**Team Lead**"

### Change WhatsApp Account Role

To change the WhatsApp account role on the profile:

* Switch to the "**WABAs Assign**" tab
* Click on the "<mark style="color:green;">**Assign WABA number**</mark>" button

<div align="left"><figure><img src="https://2303112206-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2F53n17VnOICC1LtDqlENV%2Fuploads%2FEBI9EJgHnaw5VSynjNWo%2FScreenshot%202024-03-28%20001850.png?alt=media&#x26;token=eea5a989-0a81-491e-bd0f-c8a2f6d4b5c3" alt="" width="277"><figcaption></figcaption></figure></div>

* Now, from the dropdown menu, select the WABA role you wish to assign the Team member
* Afterward, click on the "<mark style="color:green;">**Confirm**</mark>" button to save the changes

<div align="left"><figure><img src="https://2303112206-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2F53n17VnOICC1LtDqlENV%2Fuploads%2FLz2aWeYyiOd4fTn27tPv%2FScreenshot%202024-03-28%20002340.png?alt=media&#x26;token=3e09fce0-909b-4cae-82de-c43cf48b7a1e" alt="" width="277"><figcaption></figcaption></figure></div>

* The WABA role will be successfully updated
