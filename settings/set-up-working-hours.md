# Set Up Working Hours

***

## How to implement working hours?

To access the Working Hours configuration:

* Navigate to the Settings menu on the left panel
* Scroll down until you find the Working Hours section

<div align="left"><figure><img src="https://2303112206-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2F53n17VnOICC1LtDqlENV%2Fuploads%2FY9ctSrZrlUBsHXPZM6ev%2Fimage.png?alt=media&#x26;token=ba240f2b-e5d4-48f0-8d76-ad8080143edc" alt="" width="270"><figcaption></figcaption></figure></div>

* Click on Working Hours to open the configuration panel, where you can define operational hours, assign agents, and customize messages for non-working hours.

<div align="left"><figure><img src="https://2303112206-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2F53n17VnOICC1LtDqlENV%2Fuploads%2FC408dZcMMTWr4V4AUpRl%2Fimage.png?alt=media&#x26;token=98d5aec1-c5b7-42a4-ae41-18d5158cf86f" alt="" width="563"><figcaption></figcaption></figure></div>

### #1 Set Time Zone

Click on the drop-down, select the time zone that corresponds to your operational location or the region where your team functions.

### #2 Configure Working Hours

* Click on the "<mark style="color:green;">**Add Hours**</mark>" button

<div align="left"><figure><img src="https://2303112206-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2F53n17VnOICC1LtDqlENV%2Fuploads%2FGggpETpAVbR5bijzAE0m%2Fimage.png?alt=media&#x26;token=0baa7365-33f7-44b2-aaa6-bed3651673f7" alt="" width="463"><figcaption></figcaption></figure></div>

* This action will open a pop-up titled "**Add Working Hours.**"
* Select the working days by clicking on the Working hours drop down.
* Choose the start time and end time for selected working day.
* After configuring the working hours, click on the "<mark style="color:green;">**Save**</mark>" button to apply the changes

<div align="left"><figure><img src="https://2303112206-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2F53n17VnOICC1LtDqlENV%2Fuploads%2FQKwBXoDR1uvDbQmJYP98%2Fimage.png?alt=media&#x26;token=55051391-0811-41dc-b77d-b55965174798" alt="" width="563"><figcaption></figcaption></figure></div>

* The working hours for Monday have been successfully added.

<div align="left"><figure><img src="https://2303112206-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2F53n17VnOICC1LtDqlENV%2Fuploads%2FUKrDOhsuUx9Nn44wKfW9%2Fimage.png?alt=media&#x26;token=c7a159a0-bcb0-46d7-a8d5-ff3ce285dc9d" alt="" width="563"><figcaption></figcaption></figure></div>

1. Click on the "Add Hours" button.
2. Select Monday as the working day and set the start time and end time for Monday.

### #3 Working hours for special days

Specify dates for altered working hours when your team's schedule diverges from the usual daily operating hours.

* Click on "<mark style="color:green;">**Add Hours**</mark>" button.

* A "Add special working hours" pop up will appear, select Custom Start Date, Custom End Date, Start Time and End Time
* After configuring the working hours, click on the "<mark style="color:green;">**Save**</mark>" button to apply the changes

### #4 Automatically assign team members based on availability

Implement automatic assignment of team members based on their availability. Our system will identify the most suitable available person and rotate assignments among your team members using a round-robin approach.

<figure><img src="https://2303112206-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2F53n17VnOICC1LtDqlENV%2Fuploads%2FsktboJacqQUrQvktQYlX%2Fimage.png?alt=media&#x26;token=15f3f25e-065a-4d8a-be2a-78fe40252902" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

To assign team members to handle chats during working hours:

1. Click on the "Edit" button. <img src="https://2303112206-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2F53n17VnOICC1LtDqlENV%2Fuploads%2FSG5FXKDqlSHwI1nQKVKB%2Fimage.png?alt=media&#x26;token=446e7b5c-c025-4839-be7f-080ac4506b51" alt="" data-size="line">
2. A pop-up labeled "Assign To" will appear. &#x20;
3. Select the agents you want to assign for the working hours.
4. Click on the "Assign" button to confirm the assignments.

<div align="left"><figure><img src="https://2303112206-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2F53n17VnOICC1LtDqlENV%2Fuploads%2FbIrfFZQYYrxOggTwPNdF%2Fimage.png?alt=media&#x26;token=2d98185a-a447-4782-96aa-73d6b2c800d8" alt="" width="563"><figcaption><p>The selected agents will be assigned to handle chats during the designated working hours.</p></figcaption></figure></div>

### #5 Send away message for working hours

During working hours, if no team members are available to chat, an automated away message can be sent to notify customers of the unavailability.

To customize the away message for working hours:

1. Click on the "Edit" button.
2. A pop-up titled "Edit Away Message for Working Hours" will appear.
3. Enter your custom message in the provided text field.

### #6 Setup holidays

To set up holidays, access the holiday configuration. Add the dates corresponding to the holidays when your team will be off.&#x20;

<div align="left"><figure><img src="https://2303112206-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2F53n17VnOICC1LtDqlENV%2Fuploads%2FK30qf0SvFRGNXIdcTYVb%2Fimage.png?alt=media&#x26;token=1c6462e2-b182-4468-be4f-0457ed800304" alt="" width="429"><figcaption></figcaption></figure></div>

To add a holiday:

1. Click on the "Add Holiday" button.
2. A pop-up labeled "Add Holiday" will appear.
3. Enter the holiday name and date.
4. Click on the "Save" button to confirm the addition of the holiday.

#### #7 Send away message for holidays
