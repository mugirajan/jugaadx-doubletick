# How to Ensure CTWA Ads Only Generate Leads from the Facebook & Instagram Apps

When running **Click-to-WhatsApp (CTWA) ads**, you might notice that some leads come from people browsing Facebook or Instagram on a web browser (desktop or mobile browser).

üëâ **The challenge:**\
Browser users often drop off before opening WhatsApp, which reduces **lead quality** and **conversion rates**.

üëâ **The solution:**\
You can set up your Ads Manager so your CTWA ads only show inside the **Facebook and Instagram mobile apps** ‚Äî ensuring smoother redirection to WhatsApp and higher-quality leads.

This guide will walk you through the exact steps.

***

### Step 1: Open Your Campaign in Ads Manager

<figure><img src="https://2303112206-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2F53n17VnOICC1LtDqlENV%2Fuploads%2FQ4e0gTa8G2kLx2hWO3Fd%2Fimage.png?alt=media&#x26;token=7897c801-5ade-4798-8b01-e582db5cc703" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

1. Log in to **Meta Ads Manager**.
2. Go to your **CTWA Campaign**.
3. At the **Ad Set level**, scroll down to the **Placements** section.

***

### Step 2: Select Manual Placements

<figure><img src="https://2303112206-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2F53n17VnOICC1LtDqlENV%2Fuploads%2F96n0ailKAfiOyxqAYbYx%2Fimage.png?alt=media&#x26;token=34dc4fc7-90aa-46c6-ae20-505dbf52b6f1" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

* By default, Meta chooses **Advantage+ Placements** (automatic).
* Instead, select **Manual Placements** so you can choose exactly where your ad should appear.

***

### Step 3: Devices ‚Üí **Choose Mobile Only**

<figure><img src="https://2303112206-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2F53n17VnOICC1LtDqlENV%2Fuploads%2F7o91o9Tf1MySlaQWSz1h%2Fimage.png?alt=media&#x26;token=be470701-c3ab-4fd6-bfa0-036ff86405fe" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

This is the critical step you flagged.

* Under **Device settings**, select **Mobile Only**.
* If there‚Äôs an explicit option for **Mobile Web** vs **Mobile App**, make sure **Mobile App** (or ‚ÄúMobile only / App only‚Äù) is selected and **Mobile web** is deselected.
* This prevents both **desktop** and **mobile browser** users from seeing your ad.

***

### Step 4: Choose the Right Platforms

<figure><img src="https://2303112206-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2F53n17VnOICC1LtDqlENV%2Fuploads%2FpxzosV1SKCp3VP8qgNgp%2Fimage.png?alt=media&#x26;token=54ef5f99-9484-4d07-b4ff-df66e90a7674" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

* Select only **Facebook** and **Instagram**.
* Deselect **Messenger** and **Audience Network** (these bring in web traffic and third-party apps).

***

### Step 5: Pick Only In-App Placements

<figure><img src="https://2303112206-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2F53n17VnOICC1LtDqlENV%2Fuploads%2F6m2PGzzZuQrD756vGF2c%2Fimage.png?alt=media&#x26;token=d4ee67a8-3614-439b-ba74-da486b6a37bd" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

***

### üéØ Final Outcome

By following these steps:

* Your CTWA ads will only be shown inside the **Facebook and Instagram mobile apps**.
* Leads will come from people who can seamlessly click and open WhatsApp directly.
* You avoid drop-offs from **desktop** and **browser users**.
* This ensures **higher-quality leads and better conversion rates**.
