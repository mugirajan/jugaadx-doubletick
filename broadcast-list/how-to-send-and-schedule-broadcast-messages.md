## Send Broadcast Message&#x20;

*<mark style="background-color:green;">**To perform this action via API, please visit**</mark>* [*<mark style="color:red;background-color:green;">**this link**</mark>*](https://doubletick.readme.io/reference/send-broadcast-message)

To send a broadcast message, follow these steps:

* From the left panel, navigate to "**Broadcast List**."
* Select the list you want to send the broadcast message to. (Alternatively, you can create a new list and import customers into it)
* In the Broadcast List window, click on the "<mark style="color:green;">**Send Template**</mark>" button <img src="https://2303112206-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2F53n17VnOICC1LtDqlENV%2Fuploads%2FOI3Kv8KWJzaq5T7samKf%2Fimage.png?alt=media&#x26;token=160b0aef-d8ce-4101-8c43-02148d0cb36e" alt="" data-size="line">

<figure><img src="https://2303112206-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2F53n17VnOICC1LtDqlENV%2Fuploads%2FeLaIr3AEDUYfJfPv5sJv%2Fimage.png?alt=media&#x26;token=d747e9aa-20e8-499d-9374-ba6256a75493" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

* Once you click on the "Send Template" button, a pop-up "**Template**" box will appear.&#x20;
* In this box, you can search for the template you wish to send. Once you've found the desired template, click on the green arrow button <img src="https://2303112206-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2F53n17VnOICC1LtDqlENV%2Fuploads%2FxmMltxWyhdsGiJVM5c58%2Fimage.png?alt=media&#x26;token=929d4025-bff9-480d-a823-1340f794bfe5" alt="" data-size="line"> next to the template to select it for sending

<div align="left"><figure><img src="https://2303112206-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2F53n17VnOICC1LtDqlENV%2Fuploads%2FrYwRYwG0MlirNQGFb4Xm%2Fimage.png?alt=media&#x26;token=a41dfcda-744c-4fa6-b7ea-bd6f55a4083f" alt="" width="563"><figcaption></figcaption></figure></div>

* "**Add Variables to Template**" pop-up window will appear, allowing you to input or map the variables within the template.&#x20;
* Once you've completed this step, click the "<mark style="color:green;">**Next**</mark>" button to continue

<div align="left"><figure><img src="https://2303112206-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2F53n17VnOICC1LtDqlENV%2Fuploads%2FgCmBLUIO3eQ9xoOHn7tO%2Fimage.png?alt=media&#x26;token=b6073d6a-0c15-4bfd-840e-12e516a372ba" alt="" width="563"><figcaption></figcaption></figure></div>

* After configuring the template and variables, a "**Send Template**" pop-up will appear
* This pop-up will display the total count of contacts the template will be sent to
* To proceed with sending the broadcast message, click on the "<mark style="color:green;">**Send Now**</mark>" button <img src="https://2303112206-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2F53n17VnOICC1LtDqlENV%2Fuploads%2F6dI2n2vJkiuhZZfKoTEj%2Fimage.png?alt=media&#x26;token=6f7245e4-845e-4d56-89e6-f20845b0f791" alt="" data-size="line">
* This action will initiate the distribution of the template to the specified contacts

<div align="left"><figure><img src="https://2303112206-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2F53n17VnOICC1LtDqlENV%2Fuploads%2FLIleXYYEBkJKMUbuIC68%2Fimage.png?alt=media&#x26;token=cd53cb93-3b34-473d-bb2c-728d750d1f02" alt="" width="563"><figcaption></figcaption></figure></div>

* *<mark style="color:green;">**Broadcast Message sent to customer successfully !**</mark>*

<figure><img src="https://2303112206-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2F53n17VnOICC1LtDqlENV%2Fuploads%2Fl8RseWiJFvyvFETF2r2l%2Fimage.png?alt=media&#x26;token=e31250cb-d7de-4296-b475-c38a461f1718" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

## Schedule Broadcast Message

* To schedule a broadcast message for later, click on the "<mark style="color:purple;">**Send Later**</mark>" button. <img src="https://2303112206-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2F53n17VnOICC1LtDqlENV%2Fuploads%2Fdg1yxWCViaywu4d8In4C%2Fimage.png?alt=media&#x26;token=bde04193-9303-4bb4-917a-5bd2faf6a77b" alt="" data-size="line">(Check the above steps)

<div align="left"><figure><img src="https://2303112206-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2F53n17VnOICC1LtDqlENV%2Fuploads%2FTbMcYMJRnhxD7TqaXC7n%2Fimage.png?alt=media&#x26;token=f61f5cd1-c586-4e58-9bed-8b94bb6dfb09" alt="" width="465"><figcaption></figcaption></figure></div>

* A "**Send Message**" pop-up will appear. In this pop-up, click on the "<mark style="color:green;">**Select Date and Time**</mark>" button. This will allow you to choose the specific date and time for sending the broadcast message.

<div align="left"><figure><img src="https://2303112206-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2F53n17VnOICC1LtDqlENV%2Fuploads%2Fm7nz5JIJQzEarnGus7XE%2Fimage.png?alt=media&#x26;token=d3117842-9c70-4583-b5cb-0d1bd887c932" alt="" width="450"><figcaption></figcaption></figure></div>

* A pop-up calendar will appear. You can use this calendar to choose the broadcast date by clicking on the desired date.

<div align="left"><figure><img src="https://2303112206-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2F53n17VnOICC1LtDqlENV%2Fuploads%2F2OdExAKHntC8XupxMCzD%2Fimage.png?alt=media&#x26;token=ba1aa4f3-1b85-4fd5-ba00-6ead5ff38619" alt="" width="311"><figcaption></figcaption></figure></div>

* Next, select broadcast Time and click on <mark style="color:green;">**OK**</mark>

<div align="left"><figure><img src="https://2303112206-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2F53n17VnOICC1LtDqlENV%2Fuploads%2FirMQL93muD2hJOVRTHpL%2Fimage.png?alt=media&#x26;token=9154e1d0-6961-4947-82de-24b56eadbe05" alt="" width="299"><figcaption></figcaption></figure></div>
