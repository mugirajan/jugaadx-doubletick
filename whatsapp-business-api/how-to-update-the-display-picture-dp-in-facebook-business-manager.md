# How To Update the Display Picture (DP) in Facebook Business Manager

**What is a Display Picture (DP)?**\
\
A Display Picture (DP) is a profile image or avatar used to represent a user or business on digital platforms like social media, messaging apps, or online forums. It personalizes the account and makes it easily recognizable. For example: \
On WhatsApp, the DP appears as the profile image in chats. \
On Instagram, it is the circular image at the top of a userâ€™s profile. \
A DP can be a photo, logo, artwork, or any other image that represents the user or business

**Steps to Change the Display Picture from Facebook Business Manager :**

1.Log in to Facebook Business Manager: \
Access your Facebook Business Manager (FBM) account.

\
2.Access Business Settings: \
Click on the gear icon located in the top-right corner to open Business Settings.\\

3.Navigate to WhatsApp Accounts: \
Scroll down to find the WhatsApp Accounts section and click on it.\\

4.Open WhatsApp Manager: \
At the bottom of the page, click on WhatsApp Manager to proceed.

5.Locate Your Phone Number: \
In WhatsApp Manager, find your registered phone number.&#x20;

6.Go to Profile Settings: \
Next to your phone number, click on the gear icon (Settings). Select the Profile option from the menu.

<figure><img src="https://2303112206-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2F53n17VnOICC1LtDqlENV%2Fuploads%2FkKj8dxyzg5tfaNSwv0Gd%2FUntitleddesign30-ezgif.com-crop.gif?alt=media&#x26;token=f0fd8c25-fa7d-41ff-b836-a5b66c0e129c" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

7.Update the Profile Picture: \
Click on Update Profile and upload your new display picture. Save the changes to apply the update.

<figure><img src="https://2303112206-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2F53n17VnOICC1LtDqlENV%2Fuploads%2FHj2HdZy8LBFZ7TmA3BCy%2FUntitleddesign29-ezgif.com-crop.gif?alt=media&#x26;token=34c45012-2d58-46c3-a901-ca889a6a3acc" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

By following these steps, you can easily change the DP for your WhatsApp Business account through Facebook Business Manager.
