# FAQs – WhatsApp Coexistence with DoubleTick

#### **Eligibility & Setup**

1. **Can I use a regular WhatsApp account (non-business)?**\
   No. Only the WhatsApp **Business App** is eligible for Co-Existence.
2. **How do I know if my number is eligible for Co-Existence?**\
   Your number must be active on WhatsApp Business App, not flagged or banned, and successfully downgraded from the API before onboarding.
3. **What if my number is brand new?**\
   It must be active on WhatsApp Business for at least **7 days** with **real conversations** before onboarding.
4. **What’s the process for migrating from API to Co-Existence?**
   * Downgrade your number from API in Facebook Business Manager.
   * Log into the WhatsApp Business App using the same number.
   * Complete onboarding via **DoubleTick’s Embedded Signup**.
5. **Is there downtime while switching to Co-Existence?**\
   Only during the **downgrade step**. The onboarding via DoubleTick completes in just a few minutes.
6. **Will I lose my chats from DoubleTick if I downgrade and upgrade my API Number?**\
   No, your chats remain intact. However, **templates and automation settings will need to be recreated.**

***

#### **Functionality & Usage**

7. **Can I run Click-to-WhatsApp (CTWA) campaigns while using Co-Existence?**\
   Yes, you can run CTWA campaigns on the same number used for Co-Existence.
8. **Can multiple agents chat with customers at the same time?**\
   Yes. Using DoubleTick’s API tools, multiple agents can handle chats concurrently.
9. **Can automation run based on incoming messages?**\
   Yes. DoubleTick’s workflow automation works seamlessly even in Co-Existence mode.
10. **Will chatbot and automation flows work?**\
    Yes, all automation, routing, and campaign responses continue via the API.
11. **Can I assign different teams with different numbers?**\
    Yes, with **Multi-WABA deployment**, you can assign dedicated numbers for sales, support, or service teams.
12. **Can I integrate CRMs with Coexistence?**\
    Yes. All API-based integrations (like HubSpot, Zoho, or LeadSquared) continue to function normally.
13. **Is message delivery impacted?**\
    No. Message delivery remains smooth across both the App and API.
14. **Can I assign agents automatically?**\
    Yes. DoubleTick’s **Smart Routing** can automatically assign chats based on team, department, or load.
15. **Will welcome messages work?**\
    Yes, automated **Welcome Messages** can be configured on the API side.

***

#### **Features & Platform Support**

16. **Can I make voice or video calls?**\
    Yes, via the **WhatsApp Business App** only.
17. **Does Coexistence support WhatsApp Web/Desktop?**\
    Only **WhatsApp Web for Business App** is supported. The Windows Desktop App is not compatible.
18. **Are group chats and communities accessible?**\
    Yes, available via the WhatsApp Business App.
19. **Is chat history preserved?**\
    Yes, chat history remains intact across both App and API.
20. **Are disappearing messages supported?**\
    No. Disappearing messages are **disabled** for one-on-one chats.
21. **Can I view “view-once” messages?**\
    No, these are **disabled** in Coexistence mode.
22. **Can I delete messages?**\
    No. Message revoke or delete is not supported in Coexistence.
23. **Can I schedule messages?**\
    Yes, you can schedule messages via **DoubleTick’s API interface.**
24. **Can I use QR code-based chat triggers?**\
    Yes. You can generate QR codes to initiate chats directly.
25. **Are analytics and reports still available?**\
    Yes. DoubleTick provides full analytics and reporting visibility.
26. **Can I set different access levels for team members?**\
    Yes, using **User Roles and Permissions** within DoubleTick.
27. **Can I see all chats in one dashboard?**\
    Yes, all customer chats appear in DoubleTick’s **Unified Inbox.**
28. **Can agents reply from their phones?**\
    Yes, via the **WhatsApp Business App** and the **DoubleTick platform.**

***

#### **Business Tools & Catalogues**

29. **Can I create new broadcast lists in WhatsApp Business?**\
    No. Once your number is connected to DoubleTick (via Cloud API), **you cannot create new broadcast lists**. Existing broadcast lists will remain visible in the WhatsApp Business App but will become **read-only** — you can view them, but not send or edit them.
30. **Can I upload WhatsApp statuses?**\
    Yes. You can continue to upload and manage **statuses directly through the WhatsApp Business App** as before. (Posting or managing statuses isn’t supported inside DoubleTick.)
31. **Can I still use labels and catalogues?**\
    Yes. Both **Labels** and **Catalogues** remain available and manageable through the **WhatsApp Business App**. These tools are **not accessible or editable within DoubleTick**, but you can keep using them on your mobile app.
32. **Is Meta Catalogue sharing still available?**\
    Yes. You can still share your **Meta (WhatsApp-native) catalogues** from within the **WhatsApp Business App**. In addition, **DoubleTick** allows you to share **Quicksell catalogues** directly from the platform for marketing campaigns and customer engagement.
33. **Can I tag or categorize leads?**\
    Yes. DoubleTick provides **Custom Fields** and **Tags** to help you organize, segment, and manage leads effectively — a more advanced alternative to WhatsApp labels.
34. **Are chat templates supported?**\
    Yes. You can **create, manage, and send** approved **message templates** directly from DoubleTick.\
    These are required for business-initiated conversations after the 24-hour customer service window closes.
35. **Can I upload contact lists from Excel?**\
    Yes. DoubleTick supports **bulk contact upload** using Excel or CSV files, making it easy to run campaigns and manage audiences.
36. **Can I use shared inboxes?**\
    Yes. DoubleTick’s **Team Inbox** feature allows multiple team members to handle customer chats collaboratively — ensuring faster responses and better visibility.
