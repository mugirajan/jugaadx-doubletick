## Setup Analytics

To set up Analytics:

* Navigate to the "Analytics" option on the left panel.
* The panel will open, presenting two main features.
* Response Metrics and Charts

### **Response Metrics**

* To add a response metric, click on the "**Duplicate**" button on the first metric widget.
* A confirmation pop-up will appear; click "<mark style="color:green;">**Confirm**</mark>."&#x20;
* A new widget will be added to the screen.

* To select the desired data for display on the widget, click on "**Widget Settings**."
* A widget settings pop-up will appear, allowing you to choose metrics, duration, team members, and aggregation options from the dropdown menus.
* Click "Save" to apply the settings.
* You will now be able to view the saved metric data on the widget.

<figure><img src="https://2303112206-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2F53n17VnOICC1LtDqlENV%2Fuploads%2F08k1xc5zFoQHhmp8lc8p%2FUntitled%20design%20(54).gif?alt=media&#x26;token=8cbaac38-86af-4b97-b602-5ade3ddd690e" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

* To remove a widget, click on the delete icon, enabling you to delete the widget entirely

### Charts

Graphical representations displaying analytics for all conversations

* To add a chart, click on the "**Duplicate**" button on the first graph widget.
* A confirmation pop-up will appear; click "<mark style="color:green;">**Confirm**</mark>."&#x20;
* A new graph widget will be added to the screen.
