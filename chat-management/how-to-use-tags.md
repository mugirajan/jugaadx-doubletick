## How to Delete Tags?

* Click on the Tag Icon  <img src="https://2303112206-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2F53n17VnOICC1LtDqlENV%2Fuploads%2FOcdB9TzwhZub91hNR6wM%2Fimage.png?alt=media&#x26;token=733e7060-2940-4683-9886-0468eb20e26c" alt="" data-size="line"> on Chat Screen
* On click on the tag icon, an "**Add a Tag**" pop-up will appear, displaying a list of all existing tags
* Hover over the tag you wish to delete to reveal the delete icon on the right side, click on the icon&#x20;
* A delete tag confirmation will appear&#x20;
* Click on the <mark style="color:red;">**Delete**</mark> button to confirm, and the tag will be deleted
