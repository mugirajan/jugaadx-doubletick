### How to Start a direct conversation with Customer?

* Choose "chats" from the options in the left panel.
* Click on <mark style="color:green;">**New Chat**</mark> Button&#x20;
* Select Country Code & Enter the Customer's Phone Number&#x20;

<div align="left"><figure><img src="https://2303112206-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2F53n17VnOICC1LtDqlENV%2Fuploads%2FzHB7Om6SSf6qNIMt4GAk%2Fimage.png?alt=media&#x26;token=c2ba0956-9b02-4e42-8e95-183518614972" alt="" width="308"><figcaption><p>Click on Dropdown to select Country Code </p></figcaption></figure></div>

* Click on <mark style="color:green;">**Start Chat**</mark> Button&#x20;

<div align="left"><figure><img src="https://2303112206-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2F53n17VnOICC1LtDqlENV%2Fuploads%2FMDl3o4Q8TQH18sKo3kLQ%2Fimage.png?alt=media&#x26;token=5df08daf-b887-41ad-b99f-a93068aed026" alt="" width="302"><figcaption><p>Select "Cancel" to discontinue</p></figcaption></figure></div>

* The customer chat window is set to open ðŸ‘‡

<div align="left"><figure><img src="https://2303112206-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2F53n17VnOICC1LtDqlENV%2Fuploads%2FpH8NS9txi3fcwJvmiC60%2Fimage.png?alt=media&#x26;token=4e4c5606-30c3-4c86-86dd-a8db20e224f1" alt="" width="563"><figcaption></figcaption></figure></div>

* To Open Chat Window: Click on <mark style="color:green;">**Send Template**</mark> button&#x20;
* Choose the template you want to share with the customer
* Click on the arrow button <img src="https://2303112206-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2F53n17VnOICC1LtDqlENV%2Fuploads%2FVr3locyKnPHCJJsWfL9F%2Fimage.png?alt=media&#x26;token=e260c8f0-f44c-443b-8ede-dac5422d9516" alt="" data-size="line">  & select <mark style="color:green;">**Send now**</mark> &#x20;
