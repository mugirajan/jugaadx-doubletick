# How to Send Multimedia Messages

The Send Message Action feature in the WhatsApp Business API opens up a world of possibilities for businesses seeking to engage with their audience in a personalized and interactive manner.

**Multimedia Messaging Made Simple:**

With Send Message Action, you have the flexibility to include multimedia attachments such as **images, videos, and PDF files** in your messages. Whether you're sharing product visuals, instructional videos, or informative documents, these multimedia elements enhance the richness and impact of your communication.

**Versatile Use Cases:**

* **Information Sharing:** Share product images, instructional videos, or informative PDFs to provide users with detailed information about your products or services.
* **User Engagement:** Encourage users to engage with your content by sharing visually appealing images or entertaining videos that capture their interest and encourage interaction.
