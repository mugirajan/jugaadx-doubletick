# How to Connect Your Facebook Ads Insights in DoubleTick

If you are running ads on Facebook and Instagram, monitoring your campaigns is crucial to understand performance, optimize strategy, and measure ROI. DoubleTick makes this easier by allowing you to **connect your Facebook Ads Insights** directly within the platform.

This step-by-step guide is designed for users who may not be very technically sound. By the end of this guide, you’ll be able to seamlessly connect your Facebook Ads account with DoubleTick and start tracking performance.

***

### Step 1: Log in to Your DoubleTick Account

<figure><img src="https://2303112206-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2F53n17VnOICC1LtDqlENV%2Fuploads%2FG23799Mx9RZvtNXtNHFi%2Fimage.png?alt=media&#x26;token=2c2b0385-54a9-4f30-afd9-a087b87c7780" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

1. Open your browser and go to [DoubleTick](https://web.doubletick.io/).
2. Log in using your registered login number and OTP.
3. Once you’re logged in, go to the **Ads Insights** section from the left-hand menu.

***

### Step 2: Start the Connection Process

<figure><img src="https://2303112206-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2F53n17VnOICC1LtDqlENV%2Fuploads%2FxwTQQOVpwI5Dem7kySYy%2Fimage.png?alt=media&#x26;token=44f41a11-d40f-4be9-a211-af9075b9802b" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

1. Inside **Ads Insights**, click on the **Connect Account** button.
2. A new window will open asking you to sign in with your **Facebook account**.

***

### Step 3: Log in to Facebook

<figure><img src="https://2303112206-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2F53n17VnOICC1LtDqlENV%2Fuploads%2FtLvBrByqqGozohl0xXdJ%2Fimage.png?alt=media&#x26;token=d25540e6-def5-404a-9d79-ab7a17f66c5d" alt="" width="375"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

1. Enter the login credentials (email/phone and password) of the **Facebook account that manages your business assets**.
2. Click **Continue**.
3. If you’re already logged in to Facebook on your browser, it will automatically detect your account and prompt you to continue.

***

### Step 4: Select Business Account & Ad Account

<figure><img src="https://2303112206-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2F53n17VnOICC1LtDqlENV%2Fuploads%2FnN7rTasO4nnxrZsHip0O%2Fimage.png?alt=media&#x26;token=a670d5ad-f505-45f2-9607-f800ef111bfc" alt="" width="375"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

1. After login, Facebook will ask you to **choose the Business Account** you want to connect.

   * Select the account linked to your ads.

   <figure><img src="https://2303112206-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2F53n17VnOICC1LtDqlENV%2Fuploads%2FeuViXSgb6tl58jGZcbpX%2Fimage.png?alt=media&#x26;token=52832126-bec9-4526-b495-57f8925fd8cc" alt="" width="375"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>
2. Next, select the Page you want DoubleTick to fetch data from.

   * You can select one or multiple Pages depending on your needs.

   <figure><img src="https://2303112206-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2F53n17VnOICC1LtDqlENV%2Fuploads%2FkW80Frv0v4MLaFpDRKGj%2Fimage.png?alt=media&#x26;token=0090317c-c5f3-4552-a904-b99da281c442" alt="" width="375"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>
3. Next, select the **Ad Account(s)** you want DoubleTick to fetch data from.
   * You can select one or multiple accounts depending on your needs.

***

### Step 5: Grant Necessary Permissions

<figure><img src="https://2303112206-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2F53n17VnOICC1LtDqlENV%2Fuploads%2FDfcHXNYAfla9wY6dXPRW%2Fimage.png?alt=media&#x26;token=a21933c4-9c76-4b92-bb91-af5dfc2f65d2" alt="" width="375"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

To ensure DoubleTick can fetch accurate ad insights, you’ll need to grant permissions:

* ✅ **Manage Ads** – so DoubleTick can fetch data from your campaigns.
* ✅ **View Campaigns** – so you can see active and past ads.
* ✅ **Access Insights** – so DoubleTick can generate performance reports.
* ✅ **Business Settings Access** – to ensure smooth integration across accounts.

After selecting accounts and enabling permissions, click **Save,** Facebook will confirm that your account is now connected. You’ll be redirected back to **DoubleTick Ads Insights** where your ad data will start syncing automatically.

***

### Step 6: View Ads Insights in DoubleTick

Once connected, you can:

* Track **spend, reach, and engagement**.
* Monitor **campaign performance in real-time**.
* Generate reports directly from DoubleTick without switching to Facebook Ads Manager.

***

### Common Issues & Quick Fixes

* ❌ **Didn’t see your Ad Account?** – Make sure your Facebook account has **Admin or Advertiser access** to the business ad account.
* ❌ **Data not syncing?** – Recheck if all permissions (Manage Ads, View Campaigns, Insights) are enabled.
* ❌ **Multiple Business Accounts?** – Ensure you select the correct one that is actively running ads.

***

Connecting your Ads Insights with DoubleTick ensures that all your marketing performance is **centralized in one place**, making it easier to track, optimize, and grow your campaigns.

✨ With this setup, you can now analyze your **campaigns, ad sets, and ads** directly in DoubleTick and make data-driven decisions without switching platforms.
