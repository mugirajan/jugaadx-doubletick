# Deleting Customers On DoubleTick

**Overview of Deletion**\\

DoubleTick offers four methods to delete customer data, tailored to suit your needs:

1. Individual Contact Deletion
2. Multiple Selected Contacts Deletion
3. Cohort Deletion Using Filters
4. Complete Data Deletion

**Step-by-Step Guide**

**1. Individual Contact Deletion**

This method allows you to delete a single contact from your database.

a. Navigate to the Customers tab.

b. Locate and select the specific contact you want to delete.

c. Click on the Delete option.

**2.** **Deleting Multiple Selected Contacts**

You can delete several contacts at once for faster management.

a. Open the Customers tab.

b. Use the checkboxes to select the contacts you want to delete.\
\
c. Click on the Delete button.

**Important Considerations**

a. Permissions for Deletion

* The first three methods require the account owner to grant permissions to team members.
* Permissions can be configured under the Custom Roles and Responsibilities section in the settings.

b. Irrecoverable Action

* Deleted data cannot be retrieved. Ensure you double-check before proceeding.

c. Security and Compliance

* The OTP validation for the Delete All feature ensures data deletion requests are secure and authorized.
