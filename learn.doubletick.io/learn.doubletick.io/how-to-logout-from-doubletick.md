# How To Logout From DoubleTick

#### **Steps to Log Out**

1. **Access the Right Panel**
   * Open the DoubleTick application or web interface.
   * Locate the navigation panel on the **right side** of the screen.\\

2. **Scroll Down**
   * Scroll down the panel to view more options.\\

3. **Click "Log Out"**

   * Find the **"Log Out"** button or option at the bottom of the panel.
   * Click on it to sign out of your account.\\

**Step 2: Clear Cookies and Site Data**

1. Select **"Cookies and Site Data"** from the options available.
2. Navigate to **"Manage On-Device Site Data"** or a similar section where you can manage stored data.
3. Review the list of saved sites and locate any data associated with DoubleTick.\
   \\

#### **Step 3: Delete Data and Clear Cache**

1. Delete all listed sites related to DoubleTick by selecting them and clicking on the **Delete** or **Clear** option.
2. Clear the browser or app cache completely to remove any residual data.
