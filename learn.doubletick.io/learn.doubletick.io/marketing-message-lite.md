# Marketing Message Lite

**What is Marketing Messages Lite (MM Lite)?**

**Marketing Messages Lite (MM Lite)** is a new version of WhatsApp’s marketing messaging service that lets you send promotional messages **more easily**, **more affordably**, and **with better delivery rates** — all with DoubleTick's new MM Lite Feature launch.

#### **Why Should You Use MM Lite?**

* **Higher Delivery & Engagement:** Businesses in India have seen up to **9% more messages delivered**, and **better read & click rates** than with Cloud API.
* &#x20;**Cost-Effective:**  With better read rates and delivery rate, you unlock better ROI on your campaigns.
* **Built-In Ads Tracking:** Integrated with **Meta Ads Manager**, so you can link your Facebook & Instagram ads directly to WhatsApp.
* &#x20;**Quick to Set Up:** Use your **existing WhatsApp Business Account (WABA)**, phone number, and approved templates. Switching from Cloud API is easy!

<mark style="color:green;">**How to Enable and Use Marketing Messaging Lite (MM Lite) on DoubleTick?**</mark>

#### **Step 1: Look for the MM Lite Upgrade Pop-up**

1. **Log in** to your DoubleTick account.
2. Navigate to **any existing chat or broadcast**.
3. Click on **“Send Template”**.
4. Once the template preview opens, click the **green arrow** to proceed.
5. **Scroll down** in the pop-up window.
6. Look for the ***“Upgrade Delivery API to Marketing Messaging Lite API”***
7. Click on **Invite Email** in the pop-up to initiate the upgrade process.\

#### **Step 3: Send Messages with MM Lite**

1. When sending marketing  templates in DoubleTick, you’ll see a toggle for **Selected Delivery API**.
2. Select **MM Lite (Better delivery rate)** instead of **Cloud API**.
3. If the toggle is OFF, switch it ON to enable MM Lite.
4. Choose your approved templates and send your messages as usual.
