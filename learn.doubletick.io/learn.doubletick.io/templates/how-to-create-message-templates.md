## How to Create Templates?

*<mark style="background-color:green;">**To perform this action via API, please visit**</mark>* [*<mark style="color:red;background-color:green;">**this link**</mark>*](https://doubletick.readme.io/reference/create-template)

Templates are made up of four primary components which you define when you create a template: header, body, footer, and buttons. The components you choose for each of your templates should be based on

* Navigate to the left panel, select "**Templates**," and then click on the "<mark style="color:green;">**New Template**</mark>" button. <img src="https://2303112206-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2F53n17VnOICC1LtDqlENV%2Fuploads%2FlxeDNOcWE1QlRfH4JJBG%2Fimage.png?alt=media&#x26;token=3fa64e64-8444-409f-bf0d-0fcba477f404" alt="" data-size="line">

* Select "**Basic Info**," then input the template name, language, and select category. If you have multiple channels, choose the WABA number for which you want to create the template.
* &#x20;After entering the details, click on the "<mark style="color:green;">**Next**</mark>" button to proceed.

<figure><img src="https://2303112206-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2F53n17VnOICC1LtDqlENV%2Fuploads%2FFfZcadT9JBwQMKEiYyYc%2Fimage.png?alt=media&#x26;token=dda3267d-2610-4737-baaf-97b3bab51571" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

* In the content section of the template, you can input the specific details or message text that will be sent to users. This includes the ***header (optional)***, ***body (mandatory)***, which constitutes your main message, and the ***footer (optional)*** for additional information or closing remarks.
* Enter the details of the content, Once completed, click on "<mark style="color:green;">**Next**</mark>" to continue.
