## Quick Reply Buttons <a href="#quick-reply-buttons" id="quick-reply-buttons"></a>

Quick reply buttons are custom text-only buttons that immediately message you with the specified text string when tapped by the app user. A common use case is a button that allows your customer to easily opt-out of any marketing messages

*<mark style="color:green;">Templates are limited to 10 quick reply buttons</mark>*

If using quick reply buttons with other buttons, buttons must be organized into two groups: quick reply buttons and non-quick reply buttons

**Examples of valid groupings**:

* Quick Reply, Quick Reply
* Quick Reply, Quick Reply, URL, Phone
* URL, Phone, Quick Reply, Quick Reply

**Examples of invalid groupings:**

* Quick Reply, URL, Quick Reply
* URL, Quick Reply, URL

*Button label text chatracter limit: 25 characters maximum*

## Marketing-Opt Out Button

Users have the option to unsubscribe from receiving messages simply by clicking on "<mark style="color:red;">**STOP**</mark>".

To include a stop button in your template, you can easily do so by selecting the "**Add Stop Button**" option.

## Remove Button

To remove a button, simply click on the "X" sign next to it. A confirmation popup will appear, prompting you to confirm the removal. Select "<mark style="color:red;">**Remove**</mark>", and the button will be deleted.
