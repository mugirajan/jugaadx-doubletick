## **What are WhatsApp forms?**

WhatsApp forms is a powerful feature launched by DoubleTick that allows users to create custom journeys that are user-friendly and generate up to 3 times more responses and conversations.

WhatsApp forms makes it easy for your customers to get things done within the WhatsApp interface. You can design and build a variety of rich customer experiences using UI components embedded directly in your conversations.

With WhatsApp forms, you can:

* Schedule appointments
* Showcase products
* Gather customer feedback
* Generate new sales leads
* And much more, making interactions more natural and comfortable for customers

But before we get to how to make WhatsApp forms, you might beg the question - ‚ÄúWhy WhatsApp forms‚Äù?

## **Why WhatsApp Forms?**

* Intuitive - By allowing customers to fill in details within the WhatsApp interface, forms make business messaging very convenient for the end user
* Customizable - Forms can be anything you want them to be; greater customizability enables users to be more creative with forms, driving better results
* Higher Engagement - As a result of its user-friendliness, forms create better results for businesses. Meta case studies reveal that Lenovo saw 8.2 times more appointment bookings, and Brazilian lender Cons√≥rcio Magalu achieved 2.9 times more conversions by redesigning their scheduling experience using WhatsApp forms

## **How to make WhatsApp Forms** ?

**Step 1**: Navigate to your Facebook Business Manager.

**Step 2**: Go to the "Accounts" tab and select "WhatsApp Accounts."

<figure><img src="https://2303112206-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2F53n17VnOICC1LtDqlENV%2Fuploads%2FKhqi1TcrttTa7T6Z8tWb%2FGIF%201-%20Beta.gif?alt=media&#x26;token=6376587f-ef77-4b46-a5e7-18faf75157dd" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

**Step 3**: Click on the corresponding WABA account for which you want to create flows. Scroll down to open the WhatsApp Manager.

<figure><img src="https://2303112206-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2F53n17VnOICC1LtDqlENV%2Fuploads%2FsoOEgHifbaK9qWVYYmfd%2FGIF%202.gif?alt=media&#x26;token=4d989549-fde1-4682-bbbb-aeae113b7817" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

**Step 4**: In the WhatsApp Manager, on the left panel, select "Create Templates" and click on "Create a New Template."

<figure><img src="https://2303112206-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2F53n17VnOICC1LtDqlENV%2Fuploads%2Fshgf7zIq47c4knZG3ZOp%2FGIF%202%20(1).gif?alt=media&#x26;token=fbfcdd74-31fd-46bd-a9c7-fdb08b4b04ff" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

**Step 5**: Select "Flows" then click on 'Next' and assign a sample name to the template.

<figure><img src="https://2303112206-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2F53n17VnOICC1LtDqlENV%2Fuploads%2FfnOjgbzkYqWqddJFnwTH%2FGIF%204.gif?alt=media&#x26;token=b02cbdac-a2ba-48fe-99b0-cd0d510385c0" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

**Step 6:** Under the "Type of Flow" option, select "Custom Form," then click "Create."

<figure><img src="https://2303112206-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2F53n17VnOICC1LtDqlENV%2Fuploads%2Fv1f5Le4ZwGRAo2liSs7S%2FGIF%204%20(1).gif?alt=media&#x26;token=41d8857c-4283-40b4-be50-a7fb79543efb" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

**Step 7**: Add your questions to the template and click "Save."

<figure><img src="https://2303112206-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2F53n17VnOICC1LtDqlENV%2Fuploads%2F2qMFUDTCfhXQnUiIa6Ue%2FUntitled%20design.png?alt=media&#x26;token=133189e4-8ac7-438c-8589-9edebdcc2a65" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

**Step 8:** Navigate to the flows, select your flow and click on the three dots and select "Publish."

<figure><img src="https://2303112206-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2F53n17VnOICC1LtDqlENV%2Fuploads%2FKQQlAzguMAYA8IFXI5Op%2FUntitled%20design%20(1).png?alt=media&#x26;token=4db7b701-6675-4a04-ba92-1de1fd7949fe" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

**Step 9**: Once published, the flows will reflect in your DoubleTick account in the 'buttons' tab while creating a new template&#x20;
