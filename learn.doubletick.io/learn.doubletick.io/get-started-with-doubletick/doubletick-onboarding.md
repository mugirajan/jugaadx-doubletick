## **Initiate Onboarding**

Click on the "Get Started" button to commence the onboarding process.

* Upon clicking "<mark style="color:green;">**Get Started**</mark>," a pop-up window will appear. Scroll down and locate the option to "<mark style="color:blue;">**Continue with Facebook**</mark>."

<div align="left"><figure><img src="broken-reference" alt="" width="375"><figcaption></figcaption></figure></div>

* Confirm Facebook Integration; Select "<mark style="color:blue;">**Continue as**</mark>" to proceed with linking your Facebook account to DoubleTick
* Click on "<mark style="color:blue;">**Get Started**</mark>" to initiate the setup process

<div align="left"><figure><img src="broken-reference" alt="" width="375"><figcaption></figcaption></figure></div>

* **Fill in your business information** in the provided fields and click on the '<mark style="color:blue;">**Next**</mark>' button to proceed

<div align="left"><figure><img src="broken-reference" alt="" width="375"><figcaption></figcaption></figure></div>

* If you're setting up a new account, select '**Create a WhatsApp Business account**' from the dropdown menu and proceed to **Create a new WhatsApp Business profile**

<div align="left"><figure><img src="broken-reference" alt="" width="375"><figcaption></figcaption></figure></div>

* To establish a WhatsApp Business profile, first input your WhatsApp account name.&#x20;
* This name serves as internal identification and will not be visible to your customers. Next, enter your WhatsApp Business display name.&#x20;
* It's important to ensure that this display name matches your business name as it will be visible to all users on WhatsApp
* Choose the category that best represents your business, and then click on the '<mark style="color:blue;">**Next**</mark>' button to proceed

<div align="left"><figure><img src="broken-reference" alt="" width="375"><figcaption></figcaption></figure></div>

* Proceed by selecting the appropriate country code from the dropdown menu, and then enter the phone number you wish to use for the WhatsApp API.&#x20;
* *Please note that once integrated, this number will be dedicated solely for WhatsApp API use, and you will not be able to use a WhatsApp/WhatsApp Business account on this number thereafter*
* Choose your preferred method of verification: via text message or phone call.&#x20;

<div align="left"><figure><img src="broken-reference" alt="" width="375"><figcaption></figcaption></figure></div>

* You will then receive a One-Time Password (OTP) based on your selection
* Click on "<mark style="color:blue;">**Next**</mark>" to continue
