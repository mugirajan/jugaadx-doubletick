# How to Update Your Display Name On Facebook Business Account

#### **Why Update Your Display Name?**

A verified display name is visible to your customers and plays a significant role in establishing trust. You might want to update it to:

* Reflect a rebranding effort.
* Ensure consistency with your current business identity.
* Fix inaccuracies or errors in the existing name.\

**Step 1: Log in to Facebook Business Manager**

1. Open your browser and navigate to the **Facebook Business Manager** (business.facebook.com) login page.
2. Enter your credentials and log in to your account.\
   \\

   **Step 2: Access WhatsApp Settings**

   1. Once logged in, go to **Business Settings**.
   2. Under **Business Info**, click on **WhatsApp Accounts** and select your business name.
   3. Scroll to the bottom right corner and click on **WhatsApp Manager**.

**Step 3: Navigate to Account Settings**

1. In WhatsApp Manager, go to **Accounts and Tools**.
2. Click on **Phone Numbers** to access your WhatsApp phone number details.

#### **Updating Your Display Name**

1. **Access the Display Name Option**:
   * Click on the **Settings** icon in WhatsApp Manager.
   * Select **Profile** to view your current display name settings.
2. **Edit Your Display Name**:
   * Locate the **Display Name** field.
   * Click **Edit** and enter the new display name you wish to use.
3. **Submit for Review**:

   * Once you've entered the new name, click **Save**.
