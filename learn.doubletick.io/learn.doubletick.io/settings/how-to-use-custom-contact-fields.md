# How to Use Custom Contact Fields

***

Custom contact fields allow you to create tailored attributes to store specific customer data, enhancing your ability to personalize interactions and better understand your clientele.

These fields serve as customizable data points, enabling efficient organization and better understanding of individual preferences and needs.

## Edit Custom Field

\
To edit a custom contact field, click on the three dots next to the field you want to modify, then select "Edit." Make the necessary changes and click "Save Field" to confirm the updates.

<figure><img src="https://2303112206-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2F53n17VnOICC1LtDqlENV%2Fuploads%2Frfp8ONr9uIQCmcPGTXAd%2Fedit%20custom%20fields.gif?alt=media&#x26;token=79a08860-75e5-4dd8-9c66-e593cc4be3a2" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

Delete Custom Contact\\

To delete a custom contact field,&#x20;

* Click on the three dots next to the contact field you want to delete.&#x20;
* Select the "Delete" option.&#x20;
* A pop-up confirmation will appear.&#x20;
* Click on the "Delete" button to confirm. The item will then be permanently deleted.
