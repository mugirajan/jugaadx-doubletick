10 Golden Rules to Protect Your WhatsApp API Number

1️⃣ The Golden Rule: Only Message People Who WANT You ❤️

  • Imagine you walk into someone’s home uninvited… awkward, right? That’s what happens when you message people without permission.

  • Always get opt-in consent → online form, QR scan, checkout checkbox, or a “Reply YES” flow.

✅ Good Example: “Hi Riya, thanks for signing up! We’ll send you order updates and special offers on WhatsApp. Reply STOP anytime.”

❌ Bad Example: Sending a promo to a random list you bought. (Spam alert 🚨 = block + report = danger for your API).

Why this protects your number → Meta loves when users expect your messages. Zero surprises = zero complaints.

2️⃣ Play by the 24-Hour Rule ⏳

  • If a customer messages you, you’ve got a 24-hour magic window to reply with anything (free chat).

  • After 24 hours? Use an approved template only.

✅ Example: Customer says “Where’s my order?” → reply anytime in 24 hrs. ❌ Example: Sending them a discount message 2 days later without a template.

Why this protects your number → keeps you in Meta’s “green zone” and avoids penalties.

3️⃣ Templates: Dress Right for the Occasion 👗👔

Every template has a category → Utility, Marketing, or Authentication. Pick the right outfit for the right party.

  • Utility → service updates (“Your order is shipped”)

  • Marketing → promos & offers (“20% off today”)

  • Authentication → OTPs (“Your code is 54321”)

✅ Good: “Hi Aditi, your appointment is confirmed for 4 PM.” (Utility)

❌ Bad: “Hi Aditi, your appointment is confirmed. Also, here’s 20% off hair spa!” (Mixed up! Should be Marketing, not Utility).

Why this protects your number → No template mix-ups = no Meta red flags.

4️⃣ Stop Cold Broadcasting 🥶 → Start Warm Conversations 🔥

Cold blasting = 🚫. Warm, targeted audiences = ❤️.

  • Use Custom Audiences → past buyers, recent leads.

  • Use Lookalikes → people who resemble your best customers.

  • Retarget people who clicked your ads → they already showed interest.

✅ Good: Send “Thanks for shopping last month, here’s early access to our festive sale.” ❌ Bad: Blast 20,000 random numbers with “BIG SALE TODAY!!!”

Why this protects your number → Warm = fewer complaints, more replies. Cold = high spam reports → restriction risk.

5️⃣ Spice Up Your Messages 🌶️ (Content that Clicks)

  • Keep it short, friendly, personal.

  • Add value: updates, reminders, guides, deals.

  • Use media: 📸 images, 🎥 videos, 📄 PDFs, 🎯 CTA buttons.

✅ Good: “Hi Aarav! Your AC service is booked for 12 Sep. Tech: Raju (📞 9xxx). Tap below to confirm 👇 [Confirm Booking]”

❌ Bad: “AC SERVICE DISCOUNT. CALL NOW!!!”

Why this protects your number → Fun, helpful messages = happy customers = positive engagement = Meta loves you.

6️⃣ Don’t Be Pushy 🚦 (Cadence & Frequency)

  • 1-2 messages a day is more than enough.

  • Give space → follow-up after 24–72 hours if no reply.

  • Ramp campaigns slowly: 100 → 500 → 2000 (test engagement before scaling).

✅ Smart Flow: Day 1: Order update Day 3: Feedback request Day 7: Personalized offer

❌ Spam Flow: 3 promos in 1 day → instant blocks.

Why this protects your number → Prevents the dreaded “131056 error” (too many messages too fast).

7️⃣ Always Offer a Way Out 🏃‍♂️

Let users unsubscribe anytime. Simple “STOP” is enough.

✅ Good: “Reply STOP to unsubscribe. You won’t hear from us again (promise 🤞).” ❌ Bad: Keep messaging even when someone says “I don’t want updates.”

Why this protects your number → Users feel in control = fewer complaints. Meta notices when you ignore opt-outs (big danger).

8️⃣ Track Your Health 🩺

Check your Account Quality in Facebook Business Manager: Green ✅, Yellow ⚠️, Red ❌.

Also monitor:

  • Block rate → keep Quality rate high

  • Opt-out rate → keep low

  • Response rate → higher = better

  • Template approvals → if rejections pile up, rethink your wording

Why this protects your number → Early signs help you fix before Meta steps in.

🔍 How to Check Your Block Rate & Number Health in FBM

You can monitor your WhatsApp Business API number’s health directly inside Meta Business Manager (FBM). Here’s how:

🛠 Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Login to FBM Go to Meta Business Manager and sign in with your admin account.

  2. Open Business Settings On the left-hand menu, click Business Settings.

  3. Navigate to WhatsApp Accounts

    • Go to Accounts → WhatsApp Accounts.

    • Select your WhatsApp Business Account (WABA).

  4. Check Your Phone Numbers

    • Under the WABA, click on Phone Numbers.

    • Select the number you want to review.

  5. Review Number Health Here you’ll see:

    • Messaging Tier → Shows how many unique users you can reach per day (1K, 10K, 100K, unlimited).

    • Quality Rating →

      • 🟢 High (Green): Your messages are well received.

      • 🟡 Medium (Yellow): Some users are blocking you, needs improvement.

      • 🔴 Low (Red): Too many blocks or negative feedback. Immediate action needed.

    • Status → Can be Connected, Flagged, or Restricted.

📌 What Does Block Rate Mean?

Block rate is simply how many users are blocking your number after receiving your messages.

  • If too many people block → your quality rating drops.

  • If rating stays Low → Meta may restrict your number.

9️⃣ If Trouble Hits (Quality Drops or Restrictions) 🚑

Don’t panic — follow this 5-step rescue plan:

  1. Pause outbound campaigns immediately.

  2. Review what went wrong (list, template, content, frequency).

  3. Remove cold leads & fix templates.

  4. Send helpful service updates to boost engagement.

  5. Contact DoubleTick Support with logs + opt-in proof.

Why this protects your number → Shows Meta you’re proactive, reduces downtime, and helps recover faster.
