### Header <a href="#headers" id="headers"></a>

Headers are optional components that appear at the top of template messages. Enter the header information, which typically includes the title or introductory message of the template.

*<mark style="color:green;">**Templates are limited to one header component.**</mark>*

### Body <a href="#body" id="body"></a>

Body components are text-only sections that are essential in every template. They support multiple variables and have a maximum character limit of 1024.

<div align="left"><figure><img src="https://2303112206-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2F53n17VnOICC1LtDqlENV%2Fuploads%2FAhEX9nCA50CY8ioz6nLP%2Fimage.png?alt=media&#x26;token=a65e5e6a-e8b4-4801-8679-d6378b15ab72" alt="" width="541"><figcaption></figcaption></figure></div>

* Input the main content of your message in the body section. This is where you convey the primary information or message you want to communicate.
* You can incorporate custom variables within the body of your template by enclosing them in braces, like this: <mark style="color:green;">{{variable\_name}}</mark>. These variables allow for dynamic insertion of specific information when the template is used.
* You can include system [<mark style="color:green;">custom attributes</mark>](https://learn.doubletick.io/settings/how-to-use-custom-contact-fields#how-to-create-custom-contact-fields) as variables in the body by clicking on them; they are displayed in the body section highlighted in green.

### Footer <a href="#footer" id="footer"></a>

Footers are optional text-only components that appear immediately after the body component and have a maximum character limit of 60. Templates are limited to one footer component.

* Provide any closing remarks or additional details in the footer section. This could include contact information, legal disclaimers, or any other relevant information.
