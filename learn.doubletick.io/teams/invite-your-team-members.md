## Invite Team Member

To add a team member to DoubleTick, follow these steps:

* Navigate to the left panel and click on "**Teams**"
* On the Teams page, locate and click on the "<mark style="color:green;">**Invite members**</mark>" button

<figure><img src="https://2303112206-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2F53n17VnOICC1LtDqlENV%2Fuploads%2FjHYOSJ9MHhOIbON7ShKA%2Fimage.png?alt=media&#x26;token=5f8632d4-3ff0-41b6-87a1-34877f991fe3" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

* Enter the team member's name and phone number
* Choose the appropriate **Organizational role**
* Select the **WABA role**

<figure><img src="https://2303112206-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2F53n17VnOICC1LtDqlENV%2Fuploads%2F1a52MoyLlTsVdcCgn0VQ%2Fimage.png?alt=media&#x26;token=ad0781cc-ebc0-4ed8-805f-4e3ba2cbec2e" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

* Click on the "<mark style="color:green;">**Invite**</mark>" button to send the invitation

## View Pending Invites

To view pending invites on the Team page, follow these steps:

* Click on "**View ALL**" to access the pending invites page.

<figure><img src="https://2303112206-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2F53n17VnOICC1LtDqlENV%2Fuploads%2Fpp2YAnXgAZ4BjEohlg0O%2Fimage.png?alt=media&#x26;token=15a985be-72c2-44dc-8db5-3ef097fc68f0" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

* Here, you will find a list displaying the members' names, phone numbers, assigned role, and the date and time when the invites were sent to them.

## Revoke Invite

To revoke an invite on the pending invite page, follow these steps:

* Next to the member whose invite you want to revoke, click on the three dots icon
* Select "**Revoke Invite**"
* A confirmation pop-up will appear. Click on the "<mark style="color:red;">**Revoke**</mark>" button to proceed.
