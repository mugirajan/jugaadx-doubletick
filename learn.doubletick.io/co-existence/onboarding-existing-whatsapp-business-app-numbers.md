#### üîÅ For Numbers Currently on WhatsApp Business API

1. **Delete the Number from Facebook Business Manager**
   1. Log in to your **Facebook Business Manager** account.
   2. Go to **Business Settings ‚Üí WhatsApp Accounts.**
   3. Select the relevant WhatsApp Account.
   4. Click on **Settings**, then click the **Trash icon** to delete the API number.

<figure><img src="https://2303112206-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2F53n17VnOICC1LtDqlENV%2Fuploads%2FL9skwqtn7QIAWbqWyW3F%2F13.png?alt=media" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

2. **Install WhatsApp Business App and Log In using the downgraded number.**

Before downgrading number, it‚Äôs important to **back up your chats** to avoid losing any messages. Follow these steps:

2. **Open WhatsApp Business App** ‚Üí Go to **Settings** ‚Üí **Chats** ‚Üí **Chat Backup**
3. **Connect your Google Account** (if not already connected)
4. Set **Back up frequency** to **Daily**
5. Tap **Back Up Now** and wait until the upload is confirmed

‚úÖ Once the backup is complete, you can safely proceed.\\

3. **Install WhatsApp Business App and Log In**

   1. Install the **WhatsApp Business App** on your phone.
   2. Log in using the **downgraded number** (the one you just deleted from the API).
   3. Restore your chat backup when prompted.

4. **Reconnect the Number to DoubleTick**

Once your WhatsApp Business App setup is complete and active:

1. Log in to [**DoubleTick**](https://web.doubletick.io).
2. Go to **Settings ‚Üí Manage WABAs ‚Üí Add Number** (green button).\\

   ![](https://2303112206-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2F53n17VnOICC1LtDqlENV%2Fuploads%2FOY7udZsImB9ReBOZnDEk%2F14.png?alt=media)
3. Select **‚ÄúAdd number using Embedded Sign Up.‚Äù**\\

   ![](https://2303112206-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2F53n17VnOICC1LtDqlENV%2Fuploads%2FBGGibddSdG0k3CLQ2yFp%2F15.png?alt=media)
4. Choose your **Facebook Portfolio**.
5. Select **‚ÄúConnect your existing WhatsApp Business App.‚Äù**
6. Enter your number ‚Üí **Verify OTP ‚Üí Select Timezone ‚Üí Approve Access.**
7. A **QR code** will appear ‚Üí **Scan the QR** using your WhatsApp Business App.
