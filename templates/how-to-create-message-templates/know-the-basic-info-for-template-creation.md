### **Template Name:**

* The Template Name field allows businesses to assign a unique identifier to their message template
* This name can contain up to 512 characters .&#x20;
* It's important to adhere to the guidelines provided, utilizing only lowercase letters (a), numbers (0), and underscores (\_).&#x20;
* For example, a template name like "stock1\_clearance\_sale" provides a clear indication of its purpose.

### **Category:**

* The Category field offers two options: "**Marketing**" or "**Utility**." Choosing the appropriate category ensures that the template aligns with its intended purpose.&#x20;

### **Language:**

* The Language field allows businesses to specify the language of the message template from a dropdown menu. This ensures that messages are delivered in the preferred language of the target audience, enhancing user experience and comprehension.
